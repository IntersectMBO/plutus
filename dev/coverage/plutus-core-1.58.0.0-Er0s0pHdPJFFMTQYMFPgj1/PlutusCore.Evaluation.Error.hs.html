   25 {-| The type of errors that can occur during evaluation. There are two kinds of errors:
   26 
   27 1. Structural ones -- these are errors that are indicative of the _structure_ of the program being
   28    wrong. For example, a free variable was encountered during evaluation, a non-function was
   29    applied to an argument or 'tailList' was applied to a non-list.
   30 2. Operational ones -- these are errors that are indicative of the _logic_ of the program being
   31    wrong. For example, 'error' was executed, 'tailList' was applied to an empty list or evaluation
   32    ran out of gas.
   33 
   34 On the chain both of these are just regular failures and we don't distinguish between them there:
   35 if a script fails, it fails, it doesn't matter what the reason was. However in the tests it does
   36 matter why the failure occurred: a structural error may indicate that the test was written
   37 incorrectly while an operational error may be entirely expected.
   38 
   39 In other words, structural errors are \"runtime type errors\" and operational errors are regular
   40 runtime errors. Which means that evaluating an (erased) well-typed program should never produce a
   41 structural error, only an operational one. This creates a sort of \"runtime type system\" for UPLC
   42 and it would be great to stick to it and enforce in tests etc, but we currently don't. -}
   43 data EvaluationError structural operational
   44   = StructuralError !structural
   45   | OperationalError !operational
   46   deriving stock (Show, Eq, Functor, Generic)
   47   deriving anyclass (NFData)
   48 
   49 instance Bifunctor EvaluationError where
   50   bimap f _ (StructuralError err) = StructuralError $ f err
   51   bimap _ g (OperationalError err) = OperationalError $ g err
   52   {-# INLINE bimap #-}
   53 
   54 instance Bifoldable EvaluationError where
   55   bifoldMap f _ (StructuralError err) = f err
   56   bifoldMap _ g (OperationalError err) = g err
   57   {-# INLINE bifoldMap #-}
   58 
   59 instance Bitraversable EvaluationError where
   60   bitraverse f _ (StructuralError err) = StructuralError <$> f err
   61   bitraverse _ g (OperationalError err) = OperationalError <$> g err
   62   {-# INLINE bitraverse #-}
   63 
   64 instance
   65   ( HasPrettyDefaults config ~ 'True
   66   , PrettyBy config structural
   67   , Pretty operational
   68   )
   69   => PrettyBy config (EvaluationError structural operational)
   70   where
   71   prettyBy config (StructuralError structural) = prettyBy config structural
   72   prettyBy _ (OperationalError operational) = pretty operational
   73 
   74 instance
   75   (Pretty structural, Pretty operational)
   76   => Pretty (EvaluationError structural operational)
   77   where
   78   pretty (StructuralError structural) = pretty structural
   79   pretty (OperationalError operational) = pretty operational
